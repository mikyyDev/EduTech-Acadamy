Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll, simply visit our Courses page, select your desired course, and click on the “Enroll” button. You’ll then be guided through the registration process.

Are the courses self-paced?

Yes! All our courses are designed to be self-paced so you can learn at your own convenience, from anywhere, anytime.

Do I get a certificate after completion?

Absolutely. After successfully completing a course, you will receive a digital certificate that you can share on LinkedIn or include in your portfolio.

Can I access the courses on mobile?

Yes, EduTech Academy is fully responsive and mobile-friendly. You can access your courses on any device — smartphone, tablet, or desktop.

Do you offer free trial lessons?

Yes, selected courses offer free introductory lessons. You can try them before committing to the full course.

What kind of support do students receive?

Students have access to our dedicated support team, discussion forums, and mentor feedback throughout their learning journey.

Can I learn multiple courses at the same time?

Absolutely. You can enroll in as many courses as you like and manage your own study schedule across different subjects.

Are the instructors qualified professionals?

Yes, every course is taught by experienced industry experts and certified educators to ensure the highest quality learning experience.
